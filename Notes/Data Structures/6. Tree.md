### Rules

1. The right hand side child node must be greater than the parent node.
2. The left hand side child node must be less than the parent node.

## Operations 

### Balanced

1. Lookup - O(log N)
2. Insert - O(log N)
3. Delete - O(log N)

### Unbalanced

1. Lookup - O(N)
2. Insert - O(N)
3. Delete - O(N)
