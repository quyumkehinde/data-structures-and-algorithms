## Stacks and queues

A collection of data where data are arranges such that they can be accessed orderly from the first/last elemement.

## Operations on stacks

1. Push - O(1)
2. Pop - O(1)
3. Peek - O(1)
4. Lookup - O(n)

(Last In, First Out - LIFO)

## Operations on queues

1. Enqueue - O(1)
2. Dequeue - O(1)
3. Peek - O(1)
4. Lookup - O(n)

(First In, First Out - FIFO)
